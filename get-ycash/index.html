Get Ycash#

Once you download a Ycash Wallet, it's time to get yourself some Ycasn!

Sell a Gift Card for Ycash#

If you are new to cryptocurrencies, selling a gift card in exchange for Ycash is a good way to obtain some Ycash. The Private Gift uses Ycash to purchase your Amazon and Target gift cards.

Buy Ycash from a Cryptocurrency Exchange#

Most Active Exchanges#

The following exchanges have the most active markets for buying and selling Ycash:

  • Hotbit (YEC/USDT)

  • PancakeSwap (WRY/BUSD, WRY/BNB, WRY CAKE)

    • Search for the WRY contract address for Binance Smart Chain: 0x4F0c54c06bC401D02Df557FD65eE30F622155cf6

  • SafeTrade (YEC/BTC, YEC/ZEC, and YEC/ETH)

  • Citex (YEC/BTC, YEC/ETH, and YEC/USDT)
